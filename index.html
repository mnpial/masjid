About

No. 09 Arabpur Union Parishad Jame Masjid is a traditional mosque. This mosque is located next to Arabpur Union Parishad in Jessore District. Muslims from around 5 villages offer prayers here. It is a mosque with three floors. There is a well-organized committee to manage the mosque.

Committee

This mosque has a management committee consisting of 12 members. Those who assist in various developmental works of the mosque. Solves various problems of Muslims. Recently, the ablutions have been modernized and sound system installed for the convenience of worshippers.

Social Media

This mosque has a messenger group to communicate with everyone and share various information. Where various information is exchanged with the youth of the area. Special khorab is promoted here. This group is managed by Md Rocky Hossain.
